About The Founder

The driving force behind our students' success.

R. Vishakan, B.E, M.Sc (Psy.)

Founder & Chief Mentor

With over six years of dedicated teaching experience, R. Vishakan has established himself as a beacon for UPSC aspirants. His own journey, marked by successfully clearing multiple Prelims and Mains examinations and appearing for the prestigious UPSC Interview, provides him with an unparalleled, practical understanding of the entire examination cycle.

His unique academic background, combining an engineering degree with a Master's in Psychology, allows him to craft a holistic preparation strategy. This approach not only builds a strong foundation of knowledge but also equips students with the mental resilience required to excel under pressure. His popular YouTube channel is a testament to his effective teaching style, attracting students from across the country.
