About Us

The driving force behind our students' success.

R. Vishakan, B.E., M.Sc. (Psychology)

Founder-Director & Chief Mentor

With over 7 years of dedicated teaching experience, our Founder R. Vishakan, a UGC NET–qualified faculty member, is a trusted mentor for serious UPSC aspirants. Having himself successfully cleared multiple UPSC Mains examinations and appeared for the UPSC Interview, and having guided numerous aspirants through their preparation journey, he brings rare, first-hand insight into handling every stage of the Civil Services Examination—from the Prelims race pressure, to the Mains marathon stress, and finally to the calm composure required for the Interview personality test. His unique approach of blending humour with core UPSC concepts while simplifying complex topics is a hallmark of his teaching style, making learning both effective and engaging. This approach goes beyond mere content mastery to focus on mental resilience, exam temperament, consistency, and decision-making under pressure—often the decisive factors in UPSC success.

Known for his lucid explanations, practical orientation, and empathetic, student-centric mentoring, he has guided hundreds of aspirantsthrough the vast and demanding UPSC syllabus. His YouTube channel reflects his commitment to demystifying UPSC preparation and making high-quality guidance accessible to the entire aspirant community. He strongly believes that with the right guidance, sustained mentorship, and disciplined effort, cracking UPSC is no longer an insurmountable challenge.

With a prolific career spanning over four decades, Mrs. Thavamony Raman serves as the backbone of Vishakan Sir’s IAS Coaching. She brings an unparalleled wealth of experience, including over two decades in the finance sector, along with a deep passion for human excellence. Her expertise as a Psychologist enables her to provide specialized support in stress and anxiety management, behavioural and attitudinal development, all of which are essential for success in the UPSC Personality Test. At the institute, she oversees administrative excellence and financial integrity, while ensuring that every aspirant receives thoughtful guidance and professional counselling to remain resilient, focused, and confident throughout their civil services journey
